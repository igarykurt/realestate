Current Average Home Prices

As of April 20, 2025, the average home price in Spruce Grove, based on recent sales, is approximately $425,000. This figure reflects the typical cost of homes sold, making it a useful benchmark for buyers and sellers. However, current market listings show an average list price of $554,000, with a median list price of $584,950, suggesting that higher-end properties are also in demand. Here's a breakdown of price ranges:

  • Entry-level homes: Starting at around $250,000, ideal for first-time buyers or those seeking fixer-uppers.

  • Single-family homes: Typically range from $375,000 to $525,000, catering to families.

  • Luxury homes: Custom builds in upscale areas can exceed $800,000, offering premium features.

These ranges reflect the diversity of housing options, from affordable condos to spacious family homes.

Factors Influencing Home Prices

Several factors shape home prices in Spruce Grove, making it a dynamic market:

  • Location: Homes near top-rated schools like Copperhaven School or recreational hubs like the TransAlta Tri Leisure Centre often command higher prices due to their convenience and appeal.

  • Home Type: Single-family homes are pricier than townhouses or condos, while new constructions with energy-efficient features can push prices toward the higher end.

  • Size and Lot: Larger homes or those on bigger lots naturally cost more, appealing to buyers seeking space.

  • Condition: Well-maintained or recently renovated properties tend to sell at a premium compared to those needing repairs.

  • Market Conditions: With 201 active listings and an average of 51 days on the market, Spruce Grove's market is balanced, but high demand can drive prices up in certain areas.

These factors create a range of opportunities for buyers, from budget-friendly starter homes to high-end properties.

Comparison with Nearby Areas

Comparing Spruce Grove to nearby Edmonton provides valuable context. Edmonton's average home price in 2025 is around $400,000, slightly lower than Spruce Grove's $425,000. However, Spruce Grove offers a quieter, suburban lifestyle with larger lots and a strong community feel, which many buyers prioritize. Nearby Stony Plain and Parkland County have similar price ranges, but Spruce Grove stands out for its accessibility to Edmonton (a 15-20 minute commute via the Yellowhead Highway) and robust amenities, making it a competitive choice.
